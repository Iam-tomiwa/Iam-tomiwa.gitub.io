Welcome to Rose Group of Schools

Rose Group of Schools is a academic institution founded in April, 2003. Since inception, it has grown to be one of the most reputable schools in Lagos state ranked no 20 out of top 100 schools in Lagos and also ranks no. 37 out of top 100 schools in Nigeria.
Rose group of schools has 2 campuses, located at different places in Lagos. We run 3 different programmes , the first is the preschool and primary programme located in the first campus and the second programme is the secondary school and the third is the A level programme both at our second campus
We also take international exams like cambridge and SAT exams and offer scholarships to outstanding students inorder to motivate our students to pursue excellence
We involve our students in extracurricalar activities like sports, ICT skills, catering etc.

Our Mission

Our Mission is to provide a world class education using state of the art facilities, resourceful and empathetic staff whose goals will unlock the immense creativity within each child, present them with opportunities to develop & flourish to a level beyond the reach of conventional education thereby nurturing them to become confident, disciplined and exemplary global citizens.
